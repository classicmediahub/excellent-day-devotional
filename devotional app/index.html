Verse of the Day

"It is of the LORD's mercies that we are not consumed, because his compassions fail not. They are new every morning: great is thy faithfulness."

Lamentations 3:22–23 · KJV
✦ Faithfulness Mercy & Grace Old Testament
✦
Today's Reflection
Listen to Today's Devotional
Tap play to listen

Each sunrise is a covenant renewed. Before the noise of the day finds you, God has already gone before — His mercy not diminished by yesterday's failures, not strained by tomorrow's unknowns.

Jeremiah penned these words in the ruins of Jerusalem, surrounded by devastation. Yet he anchored his soul to one unshakeable truth: God's compassions are not depleted. They are replenished with every dawn.

Today, whatever you carry into this new day, lay it at His feet. His faithfulness is not a feeling that fluctuates. It is His very nature — as steady and unchanging as His holy name.

✦
Morning Prayer
Pray Together

"Lord, I receive this new morning as a gift from Your hands. Let Your mercies be the lens through which I see every challenge today. May Your faithfulness be my confidence, and Your love be my strength. Amen."
